# Weekly WhatsApp Report

**What it does:** Every Monday morning, generates a summary of your WhatsApp activity from the past week — messages sent, replies received, new leads, and pending follow-ups.

**Schedule:** Every Monday at 9:00 AM

**Cron:** `0 9 * * 1`

**Task:** `Check my WhatsApp stats for the past week: how many messages were sent, how many replies received, new leads, and any contacts that still need follow-up. Give me a brief summary.`

**Requirements:**
- WhatsApp connected via Webbai
